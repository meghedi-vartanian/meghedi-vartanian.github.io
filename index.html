About Me

I am a Clinical Psychologist and Neuroscientist with experience in MRI-based neuroimaging research, advanced statistical methods, and clinical practice. My research focuses on eating behavior, obesity, and mental health. Currently, I am a Doctoral Researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Human Cognitive and Brain Sciences and the University of Leipzig Medical Center.
