About Me

I am a Clinical Psychologist and Neuroscientist with experience in MRI-based neuroimaging research, analyzing complex data sets, and applying advanced statistical methods. My research interests include eating behavior, obesity, and mental health. I am currently a Doctoral Researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Human Cognitive and Brain Sciences and the University of Leipzig Medical Center.
